History

Developed over centuries with major contributions from the Pandya, Nayak and later rulers; closely tied to the Ramayana tradition.

Architecture

Grand Dravidian temple with towering gopurams and immense pillared corridors.

Legend & tradition

Tradition holds that Rama worshipped Shiva here to atone after the war in Lanka; the temple has 22 sacred theerthams (water bodies).

Festivals

Maha ShivaratriThai AmavasaiAadi Amavasai

Darshan & timings

Generally open from early morning to night with ritual baths at the theerthams. Confirm exact timings with the temple before visiting.

How to reach

Nearest railway station: Rameswaram. Nearest airport: Madurai (~170 km).
