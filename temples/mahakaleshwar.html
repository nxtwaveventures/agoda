History

Located beside the Rudra Sagar lake in the ancient city of Ujjain, the temple is mentioned in numerous Puranic and classical texts; the present structure dates largely to the Maratha period.

Architecture

Bhumija and Maratha architectural influences, with the sanctum set below ground level.

Legend & tradition

The lingam is revered as Dakshinamurti, uniquely facing south, a feature considered especially auspicious here.

Festivals

Maha ShivaratriNag PanchamiKumbh Mela (Simhastha)

Darshan & timings

Opens very early for the Bhasma Aarti (pre-dawn) and remains open through the day. Bhasma Aarti requires advance booking; confirm with the temple.

How to reach

Nearest railway station: Ujjain Junction. Nearest airport: Indore (~55 km).
