History

The temple combines older shrine elements with Nagara additions from the 18th century; the surrounding hills are now a wildlife sanctuary.

Architecture

Nagara-style stone temple set amid dense Western Ghats forest.

Legend & tradition

Associated with Shiva's defeat of the demon Tripurasura, after which he is said to have rested here.

Festivals

Maha ShivaratriKartik Purnima

Darshan & timings

Generally open from early morning to night with regular aartis. Confirm exact timings with the temple before visiting.

How to reach

Nearest railway station: Pune. Nearest airport: Pune (~110 km).
