History

Deoghar ('abode of the gods') has long been a pilgrimage centre; the temple complex includes numerous smaller shrines around the main sanctum.

Architecture

A cluster of stone temples with the main shrine topped by a tall shikhara.

Legend & tradition

Linked to the legend of Ravana offering his heads to Shiva; the lingam is also called Kamna Linga, said to fulfil devotees' wishes.

Festivals

Maha ShivaratriShravani Mela (Kanwar Yatra)

Darshan & timings

Generally open from pre-dawn to night; the Shravani Mela period sees very heavy footfall. Confirm timings before visiting.

How to reach

Nearest railway station: Jasidih (~7 km). Nearest airport: Deoghar / Patna / Ranchi.
