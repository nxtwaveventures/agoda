History

The present temple was built in the 18th century by Peshwa Balaji Baji Rao (Nana Saheb) in black basalt.

Architecture

Ornate Nagara-style temple in black stone with finely carved mandapas.

Legend & tradition

The lingam here is distinctive for its three faces representing Brahma, Vishnu and Shiva.

Festivals

Maha ShivaratriKumbh Mela (Simhastha)Tripuri Purnima

Darshan & timings

Generally open from early morning to night with regular aartis. Confirm exact timings with the temple before visiting.

How to reach

Nearest railway station: Nashik Road. Nearest airport: Nashik / Mumbai.
