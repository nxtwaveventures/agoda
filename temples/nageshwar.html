Nageshwar Temple

A Jyotirlinga near Dwarka, traditionally identified with the ancient forest of Daarukavanam.

Primary deity
Shiva (Jyotirlinga)
Location
Near Dwarka, Gujarat
Category
Jyotirlinga
Coordinates
22.336, 69.086
View on map →

History

A long-revered Shiva site near the Saurashtra coast; the complex is marked by a very large seated statue of Shiva visible from afar.

Architecture

A modern temple structure over the ancient lingam, with a tall Shiva murti in the precinct.

Legend & tradition

Associated with the legend of Shiva protecting the devotee Supriya from the demon Daaruka.

Festivals

Maha ShivaratriKartik Purnima

Darshan & timings

Generally open from early morning to night with regular aartis. Confirm exact timings with the temple before visiting.

How to reach

Nearest railway station: Dwarka. Nearest airport: Jamnagar.
