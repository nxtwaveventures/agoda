History

Set in the Nallamala hills on the banks of the Krishna river, the temple complex carries inscriptions and additions from successive dynasties including the Reddy and Vijayanagara rulers.

Architecture

A large fortified complex with carved stone gopurams and mandapas in the Dravidian tradition.

Legend & tradition

Associated with the legend of Shiva and Parvati following their son Kartikeya to the Srisailam hill.

Festivals

Maha ShivaratriUgadiDussehra

Darshan & timings

Generally open from early morning to night with multiple sevas. Confirm exact timings with the temple before visiting.

How to reach

Nearest railway stations: Markapur and Nandyal. Nearest airport: Hyderabad.
